Common MUN Committees

United Nations Security Council (UNSC)

The UNSC is responsible for maintaining international peace and security. It discusses threats to peace, recommends actions, and can authorize military intervention.

United Nations General Assembly (UNGA)

The UNGA provides a platform for all member states to discuss international issues, adopt resolutions, and address global challenges collaboratively.

Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC)

ECOSOC focuses on economic, social, and environmental issues, promoting international cooperation in these areas and working towards sustainable development.

Human Rights Council (UNHRC)

The UNHRC is responsible for promoting and protecting human rights around the world, addressing violations and reviewing human rights situations.

International Court of Justice (ICJ)

The ICJ settles legal disputes between states and gives advisory opinions on legal questions referred to it by the UN and its specialized agencies.

Disarmament and International Security Committee (DISEC)

DISEC addresses issues related to disarmament and international security, working to promote peace and security through dialogue and cooperation.

Special Political and Decolonization Committee (SPECPOL)

SPECPOL deals with a range of political issues, including decolonization, the advancement of women, and peacekeeping efforts worldwide.

Global Health Committee

This committee focuses on health-related issues on a global scale, addressing pandemics, health policies, and initiatives to improve global health standards.
